Delicious weiners

Hot dogs are cylindrical casings filled with ground meat.

Ingredients

  • Hotdogs
  • Buns
  • Toppings (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cook hotdogs via boiling, frying, microwaving, or grilling.
  2. Place a cooked hotdog into a bun.
  3. Top with toppings to your preference.
