Delicious Burgers

Burgers are a beef patty between two buns.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Buns
  • Toppings (Optional)

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into flat patties.
  2. Cook shaped patties via frying or grilling.
  3. Place a cooked patty on a bun.
  4. Top with toppings to your preference.
